Tiny Years:
Description:
Tiny Years is an app where you add collection of groups, add data, Memories about your requirement what you want to add regarding it.

Features:
•	Add Groups into the App.
•	User Interaction & Support.
•	Delete the images/groups, Make Favourites which group you want. 
•	Add Images by using File Picker (Browse the Images from your directory). 
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
